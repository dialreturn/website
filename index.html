FAQ

Questions before you answer the next call?

Does DialReturn replace my phone system?

No. DialReturn is designed to sit alongside your existing phone and lead systems, then trigger recovery workflows when an opportunity is missed.

Can we customize the SMS copy?

Yes. Workflows should match your brand voice, use clear sender identity, and include opt-out instructions where appropriate.

Who is DialReturn best for?

Teams that rely on inbound calls, appointment requests, or lead forms, especially where response speed directly affects booked revenue.

How quickly does follow-up happen?

Most teams start with immediate first-touch SMS, then add timed follow-ups based on business hours, lead source, and the urgency of the inquiry.
