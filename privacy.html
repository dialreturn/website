Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

DialReturn (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides tools that help businesses recover missed calls and follow up with inbound leads. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through our website and services.

Information We Collect

When you submit a form on our website, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business or company name
  • Message or inquiry details

We also automatically collect basic technical data such as browser type, device type, and general usage analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or demo request
  • Provide customer support
  • Set up follow‑up workflows if you become a customer
  • Improve our website and services

SMS Messaging & Compliance

If you provide a phone number, you may receive SMS messages related to your inquiry or account. By submitting your phone number, you consent to receive these messages.

Standard message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

Where Your Data Is Stored

We use trusted third‑party processors to store and manage form submissions:

  • Google Forms / Google Sheets — form submission storage
  • Make.com — workflow automation
  • Twilio — SMS delivery (for customers)

These services process data on our behalf and follow their own security and privacy standards.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain form submissions for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or maintain business records.
